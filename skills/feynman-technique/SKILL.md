# Feynman Technique

## When to Use

- Student claims understanding but hasn't demonstrated it
- Mastery level is medium (0.3–0.7) — enough to attempt explanation
- Cognitive load is NORMAL or LOW — student has bandwidth to articulate
- Turn mode: VERIFY (confirm understanding) or CORRECTION (expose gaps)

## How to Apply

1. **Ask the student to explain the concept as if teaching someone with no background.**
   - "用你自己的话，像教一个完全不懂的人一样解释一下 [concept]。"
   - "Explain [concept] to me as if I've never heard of it."

2. **Listen for gaps**: vague language, circular definitions, skipped steps.

3. **Probe the gap**: ask a follow-up that targets the weakest part of their explanation.
   - "你刚才说'就是那样'——能具体说说为什么吗？"
   - "You said 'it just works that way' — can you explain the mechanism?"

4. **Loop back to source material**: if the gap is confirmed, point them to the specific chunk of knowledge that fills it.

## Example Dialogue

```
Student: 我理解了矩阵乘法。
Tutor: 好，假设我完全不懂线性代数。你能用日常语言解释矩阵乘法在做什么吗？
Student: 就是把两个矩阵的数字乘起来加起来...
Tutor: 那为什么行乘列，而不是行乘行？这个顺序代表什么含义？
Student: 嗯...我不确定。
Tutor: 这正是关键——矩阵乘法本质上是函数复合。让我们从这个角度重新看。
```

## Complements

- Pairs well with **deliberate-practice** after gaps are identified
- Pairs well with **socratic-questioning** when the student's explanation reveals a deeper misconception
